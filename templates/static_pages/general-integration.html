Integration Guidelines


General Integration Process:


Please follow the below steps for integration:

1. Fill in the desired Tag name, Tag attr and Tag attr value and click "Save and continue editing" in the botton right corner of the page.

2. Then click on "Get Content Area" in the upper right corner and check whether the system is able to fetch the news present in the Web URL.

3. If not then repeat the whole process from step 1 by trying different Tag name, Tag attr and Tag attr value and check again.

4. If the system is showing the desired content in "Get Content Area", then check the "Overwrite" box and click "Save and continue editing".


Do's and Dont's:

Please do not check the "Overwrite" box untill and unless you are not geting any content while clicking on "Get Content Area."
