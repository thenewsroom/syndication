Integration Guidelines


Integrating New AIDs:


1. Check for news title:

Try to integrate news title in the web URL. If the news title cannot be integrated, then move to step 2.

2. Check for news date:

Try to integrate news date in the web URL. If the news date cannot be integrated, then move to step 3.

3. Check for full news page:

Try to integrate full news page in the web URL. If full news page cannot be integrated, then put "Cannot Integrate" in your report's comments section.
