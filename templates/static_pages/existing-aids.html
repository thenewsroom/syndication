Integration Guidelines


Cheking Existing AIDs:


Please follow the below steps:

1. Click on "Get Content Area" and check whether the news present in the Web URL, "Get Content Area" and "New Snapshot" is the same. If not then follow step 2 else follow step 3.

2. Repeat all steps mentioned in guidelines for "General Integration Process" and put "Fixed" in your report's comments section after completing the steps as mentioned.

3. If content present in the Web URL, "Get Content Area" and "New Snapshot" is the same, then don't click on anything and just put "Working Fine" in your report's comments section.


Do's and Dont's:

Please do not check the "Overwrite" box if the story in snapshot is greater than or equal to 72 hours from the latest story in Web URL.

Please do not click on "Save and continue editing" if the news in the Web URL, "Get Content Area" and "New Snapshot" is the same
